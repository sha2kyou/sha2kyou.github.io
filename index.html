FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Sidefy features and functionality.

What information sources does Sidefy support?

Sidefy supports a wide range of information sources including system calendars, popular task managers, weather services, system status indicators, and custom plugins. The plugin system allows for virtually unlimited extensibility.

Is my data secure?

Yes, absolutely. Sidefy processes all data locally on your Mac. No information is sent to external servers unless specifically configured by you through plugins that require it (like weather data).

How do I create custom plugins?

We provide comprehensive documentation and a plugin development kit. You can create plugins using JavaScript/TypeScript with access to system APIs for calendars, notifications, and more.

What macOS versions are supported?

Sidefy supports macOS 11.0 (Big Sur) and later versions. The app is optimized for both Intel and Apple Silicon Macs.

Can I customize the appearance?

Yes, Sidefy offers extensive customization options including themes, layout arrangements, widget sizes, and display preferences to match your workflow and aesthetic preferences.

How do I get support?

You can reach out through our support email at sha2kyou@outlook.com or visit our GitHub repository for community support and plugin development resources.
