Pricing FAQ

Common questions about Sidefy pricing and features

What's the difference between Lite and Pro?

Lite version includes minimal bubble mode, detailed bubble mode, keyboard-only and mouse operation modes, plus Calendar and Reminders plugins. Pro version adds RSS, GitHub, custom plugins, online plugin store, more theme modes, iCloud sync, and priority support.

Is Pro a subscription or one-time purchase?

Pro is a one-time purchase for $4.99. Once you buy it, you own all Pro features forever with no recurring charges.

Are Lite and Pro the same app?

No, Lite and Pro are separate applications. If you need Pro features, you can access the purchase page through the Pro link in the Lite app, or search and download Sidefy directly from the Mac App Store.

What payment methods do you accept?

All payments are processed through the Mac App Store, which accepts credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and App Store gift cards.
